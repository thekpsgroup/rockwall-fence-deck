Questions

Fence Repair FAQs

Should I repair or replace my fence?

If the posts are sound and only sections are damaged, repair is the smart move. If posts are widely rotted and panels are failing throughout, replacement is usually more cost-effective long-term. We'll give you an honest call after seeing photos.

Can you match my existing fence?

In most cases yes — we match cedar and picket style as closely as possible. New boards will weather to match the rest over time.

Do you handle storm-damage repairs quickly?

Yes. After Texas storms we prioritize getting fences secure and functional again as fast as we can.
