Questions

Good to Know

How much does a new cedar fence cost?

In our area, cedar privacy fences typically run about $35–$55 per linear foot installed — so a standard 150-ft backyard runs roughly $5,000–$8,000 depending on height, grade and gates. Send your details for an exact written quote.

How much does a new deck cost?

Most wood decks here run about $30–$60 per square foot built, depending on size, height, material and railings. We'll give you a clear written price after a quick look at your space.

How long does a project take?

Most residential fences go up in 1–3 days; decks usually take a few days to a week depending on size and design. We give you a realistic timeline with your quote.

Do you handle repairs, or only new builds?

Both. We repair leaning fences, broken pickets, rotted posts and storm damage, and we restore weathered decks with cleaning, sanding and fresh stain.
