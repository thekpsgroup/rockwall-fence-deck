Questions

Fence Installation FAQs

How much does a cedar fence cost?

In our area, cedar privacy fences run about $45–$65 per linear foot installed. A standard 150-ft backyard typically lands between $6,500 and $10,000 depending on height, cedar grade and gates.

How long will a cedar fence last?

With proper posts and quality cedar, a privacy fence commonly lasts 15–20+ years. Staining it every few years extends life and keeps it looking new.

Do you handle property lines and HOA rules?

We help you confirm the layout and build to common HOA specs. You're responsible for confirming exact property lines, and we'll flag anything that looks off before we dig.

Will you remove my old fence?

Yes — tear-out and haul-off of the old fence is included in your estimate.
