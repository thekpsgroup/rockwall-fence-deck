Fences & Decks in Poetry, TX

Poetry is a historic unincorporated rural community in northern Kaufman County, founded in 1845 as Turner's Point and renamed Poetry in 1876 when a local merchant said the springtime countryside reminded him of a poem. It remains a small farmland-and-acreage community at the crossroads of FM 986 and FM 1565. Fence & deck contractor in Poetry, TX

Larger rural lots and ranchettes in Poetry often call for ranch-style pipe, wire or cedar perimeter fencing rather than the close-board privacy fencing common in suburban subdivisions. On every Poetry job we set posts deep in concrete, use quality cedar and coated fasteners, and build decks on properly sized footings — so your fence stays straight and your deck stays solid through North Texas heat, storms and the region's shifting Blackland clay soil.
