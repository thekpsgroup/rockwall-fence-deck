Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This page explains what information Rockwall Fence & Deck collects through this website and how we use it. We keep it simple: we only use your information to prepare your estimate, do the work, and stay in touch with you about it.

What we collect

  • Details you give us through the quote form — your name, phone number, email, property address, the work you need, project size, timeline, any notes, and any photos you choose to upload.
  • Basic website data — pages you visit, and general device and browser information, collected through Google Analytics to help us understand how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To prepare and send you a written estimate.
  • To schedule and carry out the work, and to contact you about your project.
  • To improve the website.

We do not sell your information, and we don't share it for anyone else's advertising.

Who we share it with

We use a few trusted services to run the site and handle your request. They only receive what they need to provide their service:

  • Resend — delivers the quote-form email to us.
  • Vercel — hosts the website.
  • Google Analytics — measures website traffic.

We may also share information if the law requires it.

Cookies & analytics

Google Analytics uses cookies to measure visits. You can block cookies in your browser, or opt out of Google Analytics with the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

How long we keep it

We keep quote requests for as long as we need them to do the work and for our normal business records.

Your choices

You can ask us to see, correct, or delete the information you've given us — just call or email and we'll take care of it.

Children

This website is meant for homeowners and isn't directed at children under 16.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll change the date at the top of this page.

Contact us

Questions about your information? Call (469) 887-1100 or email quotes@rockwallfenceanddeck.com.
